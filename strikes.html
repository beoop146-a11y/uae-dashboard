UNITED ARAB EMIRATES 2 STRIKES

MAR 2, 22:40 UTC INTERCEPT

Dubai Missile Interception

Emirati air defense successfully intercepted incoming ballistic missile over Dubai. Debris from interception fell in Al Barsha and Business Bay areas. Three civilian casualties confirmed, multiple injuries reported.

Impact: DXB operations suspended 4 hours. Sheikh Zayed Road closed temporarily.
MAR 1, 14:32 UTC STRIKE

Abu Dhabi Naval Base Attack

Two Iranian Shahed-136 drones penetrated UAE airspace and struck Al Salam Naval Base. Four military personnel killed. Secondary fires reported. Etihad Airways headquarters evacuated as precaution.

Impact: AUH closed 8 hours. UAE raised threat level to maximum.
